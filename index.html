About Me

I am a mobile application developer with ~4 years of professional experience building scalable, user-centric applications. My expertise spans Flutter SDK (3+ years), delivering cross-platform apps with responsive UI, state management using Riverpod and GetX, and integration of Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions) and Google Cloud services (Maps SDK, Places API, Geocoding API, Location Services).

On the native side, I have hands‑on experience with Kotlin and the Android SDK, crafting modular architectures, leveraging Jetpack components, and optimizing performance for Android devices. I also bring knowledge of Java, Spring Boot, and CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions to ensure robust delivery.

My focus is on clean architecture, responsive design, and delivering polished mobile experiences that balance performance with usability, making apps production‑ready and industry‑standard.
